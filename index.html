The Berry Ratio in Transfer Pricing

The Berry Ratio is a critical financial ratio used to determine if a taxpayer’s transfer price is at arm’s length. It is particularly effective for distributors and service providers who do not perform significant unique functions.

Berry Ratio = Gross Profit / Operating Expenses

Understanding this ratio is vital for benchmarking routine distribution activities in international transactions.
