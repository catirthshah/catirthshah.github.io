About

I am a Chartered Accountant with focused professional practice in direct taxation, international taxation, and GST, with a strong emphasis on tax litigation support and advisory matters.

My work involves independent handling of tax notices and proceedings, detailed analysis of assessment orders, identification of disputed additions, and drafting of structured, legally sustainable replies supported by statutory provisions and judicial precedents.

In international taxation, I work on DTAA interpretation, income characterisation (FTS, royalty, business income), withholding tax advisory, and transfer pricing–related matters including benchmarking concepts and Form 3CEB exposure.
