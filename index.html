About

I am a Chartered Accountant with focused professional practice in direct taxation, international taxation, and GST, with particular emphasis on tax litigation support and advisory matters involving complex legal and factual issues.

My professional foundation includes over three years of structured, hands-on training during mandatory articleship, where I worked extensively on income-tax and GST matters, including analysis of assessment orders, drafting of replies to statutory notices, and preparation of legally sustainable submissions supported by statutory provisions and judicial precedents.

Building on this training, I now provide independent advisory and drafting support, assisting clients in navigating disputes, compliance challenges, and interpretational issues under Indian tax laws.

In international taxation, my work involves treaty interpretation, income characterisation, withholding tax analysis, and transfer pricing-related matters. I am currently pursuing the ADIT (Advanced Diploma in International Taxation – UK) to remain aligned with evolving cross-border tax frameworks.

I primarily assist High-Net-Worth Individuals (HNIs), expatriates with cross-border tax exposure, and mid-sized businesses requiring structured tax advisory and litigation support.

Areas of Practice

Tax Litigation & Notices

Defending clients against income-tax and GST notices, handling reassessment and scrutiny proceedings, and addressing issues relating to unexplained cash deposits, investments, and asset-related additions through clear, structured replies.

Direct Tax Advisory

Advisory on income-tax computation, disputed issues, interpretation of complex provisions, and preparation of assessment and appellate documentation to reduce litigation risk.

International Taxation

Advisory on cross-border transactions, treaty benefits, income characterisation (FTS, royalty, business income), withholding tax analysis, and transfer pricing-related compliance exposure.

GST Advisory

GST return compliance, ITC reconciliation, resolution of ITC mismatches, handling of GST notices, and advisory on export-related GST matters including LUT and refunds.
