Insights

The Berry Ratio in Transfer Pricing

The Berry Ratio is used in transfer pricing to evaluate whether a distributor earns appropriate returns relative to operating expenses.

Berry Ratio = Gross Profit / Operating Expenses

It is most relevant where the tested party performs routine distribution functions without significant inventory or intangibles.
