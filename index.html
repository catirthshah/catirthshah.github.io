About

I am a Chartered Accountant with focused professional practice in direct taxation, international taxation, and GST, with particular emphasis on tax litigation support and advisory matters.

My work involves end-to-end handling of tax notices and proceedings, including detailed analysis of assessment orders, identification of disputed additions, and preparation of structured replies supported by statutory provisions, judicial precedents, and factual analysis.

In international taxation, I work on treaty interpretation, income characterisation, withholding tax analysis, and transfer pricing-related matters, including benchmarking concepts and Form 3CEB exposure.

My approach is technical, documentation-driven, and defensible, focusing on risk assessment, legal sustainability, and clarity of tax positions rather than generic compliance.
