The Berry Ratio in Transfer Pricing

The Berry Ratio is a financial indicator used in transfer pricing analysis to determine whether a distributor earns an appropriate return relative to operating expenses.

Berry Ratio = Gross Profit / Operating Expenses

The concept originated in the DuPont case and is particularly relevant when analysing limited-risk distributors performing routine marketing and distribution functions.
